What is permanent jewelry?

Worn Without
a Clasp.

A delicate chain welded directly onto your wrist, ankle, or finger — no clasp, no fuss. Jewelry that stays with you through every chapter of your story.

Myth

"It's stuck on forever — you can never take it off."

M
✦ Fact

Clasp-free, not life-sentenced. Simple scissors remove it anytime — MRI, surgery, or a new vibe.
